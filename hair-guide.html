Hair Care Tips

Washing

Use cool water and sulfate-free shampoo. Wash gently with downward strokes. Avoid twisting or wringing. Rinse thoroughly until water runs clear.

Styling

Use low heat when possible. Always apply heat protectant spray first. Allow hair to air dry partially before styling. Avoid excessive straightening or curling.

Sleeping

Braid your hair before bed to prevent tangling. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction. Keep hair moisturized with leave-in conditioner at night.

Swimming

Wet hair with clean water before entering pool or ocean to prevent chemical absorption. Rinse immediately afterward. Apply deep conditioner the same day to restore moisture.

Conditioning

Deep condition weekly to maintain moisture and shine. Focus on mid-lengths and ends. Leave deep conditioning mask on for 15-30 minutes for best results.

Storage

Store extensions in a cool, dry place when not wearing. Use a wig stand or hang them to prevent tangling. Keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources.
