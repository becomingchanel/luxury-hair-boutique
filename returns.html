Returns & Exchanges

We stand behind our premium hair. 14-day returns with full satisfaction guarantee.

14-Day Return Window

Simple 14-Day Return Policy

You have 14 days from the date of delivery to return your hair extensions for a full refund. No questions asked. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we make the return process easy and hassle-free.

Your satisfaction is our priority. We want you to feel confident in your purchase, which is why we offer this generous return window. If the texture, length, or quality doesn't meet your expectations, simply start a return and we'll take care of the rest.

Conditions for Returns

To qualify for a return, your hair must meet these conditions:

  • Unopened and in original, sealed packaging
  • All tags attached and intact
  • No signs of use, installation, or styling
  • No damage, matting, or tangling
  • Hair bundle weight not altered
  • Return initiated within 14 days of delivery

Important Note

Hair that appears used, styled, installed, or damaged may not qualify for return. We inspect all returns to ensure quality standards. If your hair has been opened or used in any way, we may not be able to process the return.

Exchanges

Not sure about your selection? No problem! We offer exchanges for unopened items within 14 days of delivery. Swap your bundle for a different texture, length, or color at no additional cost (if exchanging for equal value).

Different Texture

Change from straight to wavy, curly, or coily if you prefer a different look.

Different Length

Swap for a longer or shorter bundle to better suit your needs.

Different Color

Exchange for a different shade if you need a color match adjustment.

Contact our customer service team at exchanges@luxuryhairboutique.com to initiate an exchange. We'll help you find the perfect alternative.

Refund Timeline

Refund Processing: 5-7 Business Days

Once we receive your return and verify the condition meets our standards, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days. Depending on your bank, funds may take an additional 1-3 business days to appear in your account.

  1. Return Initiated: Contact us and receive prepaid return shipping label
  2. Package Sent: Ship your unopened bundle back to us using the provided label
  3. In Transit: Your package is on its way to our facility (typically 3-5 days)
  4. Received & Inspected: We receive your return and inspect it (1-2 business days)
  5. Refund Processed: Upon approval, refund is issued within 5-7 business days
  6. In Your Account: Funds appear (additional 1-3 business days depending on your bank)

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact Us: Email returns@luxuryhairboutique.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. Receive Label: We'll send you a prepaid return shipping label (no cost to you)
  3. Pack Your Hair: Place your unopened bundle in original packaging with all tags attached
  4. Ship It Back: Use the prepaid label to ship your return to us
  5. We Inspect: Upon receipt, we'll verify the condition and quality
  6. Get Your Refund: Once approved, your refund is processed within 5-7 business days

Need Help?

Contact our customer service team at returns@luxuryhairboutique.com or support@luxuryhairboutique.com. We're here to help make the return process as smooth as possible.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

Final Sale Items

Clearance bundles and final sale items cannot be returned.

Custom Orders

Custom bundles made specifically for you are non-returnable.

Used Items

Hair that has been opened, installed, or used cannot be returned.

Check Product Listing

Non-returnable items will be clearly marked on the product page at time of purchase. Be sure to review the product details before ordering if you have concerns.
