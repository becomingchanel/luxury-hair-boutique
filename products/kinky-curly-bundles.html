Experience the Natural Luxury of Raw Kinky Curly Human Hair
Our Kinky Curly Bundles are 100% raw human hair, single donor, and cuticle-aligned. These premium extensions maintain their natural kinky curly pattern and last 2-5+ years with proper care. Can be colored and heat-styled for ultimate versatility.
What Makes Raw Kinky Curly Hair Special

  • Cuticle-aligned strands prevent tangling and matting
  • 100% unprocessed means no chemical treatments
  • Defined kinky curl pattern is easy to maintain
  • Can be dyed, highlighted, and heat-styled
  • Lasts 2-5+ years with proper care

Washing Your Raw Kinky Curly Hair
  • Use sulfate-free, moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Co-wash method: cleanse with conditioner only, 1-2 times weekly
  • Rinse with cool water to seal the cuticles
  • Never use hot water or harsh shampoos
  • Allow hair to air dry to preserve curl pattern
Styling Tips
  • Use curl-defining creams to enhance natural curls
  • Apply leave-in conditioner for moisture and definition
  • Diffuse dry for best results
  • Deep condition weekly for optimal moisture
  • Use a silk pillowcase to minimize friction
