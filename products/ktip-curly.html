Our premium K-Tip Curly Extensions feature luxurious curly texture with pre-bonded keratin tips for seamless, professional installation. Made from 100% raw human hair, these extensions deliver stunning definition and bounce while remaining gentle on your natural hair.

The salon-grade keratin bonds are reusable and can be reset up to 3-4 times, making these extensions a smart, economical investment. Each bond is perfectly formulated to hold the curls in place while providing a lightweight, discreet application. Bonds are virtually undetectable for a completely natural look.

With proper care, K-Tip Curly Extensions last 3-6 months per application. The curl pattern remains beautiful and intact throughout wear. Perfect for clients who love texture and want to enhance their natural curl pattern with minimal damage.

Installation: K-Tips should be professionally installed by a certified technician. The keratin bonds are applied with a precision fusion tool. Installation typically takes 3-5 hours depending on desired fullness and curl preservation.

Washing: Wait 48 hours after installation before washing. Use sulfate-free, curl-friendly shampoo and conditioner. Wash in cool water to preserve curls. Apply conditioner generously, especially to the curl ends.

Curl Maintenance: Use curl creams and defining products to enhance and maintain the curl pattern. Apply products to damp hair and scrunch upward. Avoid touching curls while they're setting. Use a bonnet or silk scarf when sleeping.

Professional Maintenance: Get maintenance appointments every 6-8 weeks. Bonds loosen naturally as your hair grows. Professional maintenance includes curl refreshing, re-tipping, and repositioning.

Shipping: All orders ship within 2-3 business days. Domestic orders arrive within 5-7 business days. International shipping available to select countries.

Returns: We offer a 30-day return policy. K-Tips must be unused and in original condition. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Installation Services: We recommend professional installation for best results. Contact us for certified installer recommendations in your area.

How to Measure: Measurement is taken from the top of the root to the tip of the hair when laid flat and stretched out.

16" = shoulder length | 18" = mid-back | 20" = bra strap | 22-24" = mid-back length | 26" = full back length

Application Guide: K-Tips are applied to sections of your natural hair using fusion technology. A full head typically requires 100-150 tips. The number may vary based on desired fullness and hair texture.
