Washing Your Raw Wavy Hair
  • Use sulfate-free, moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Co-wash method: cleanse with conditioner only, 1-2 times weekly
  • Rinse with cool water to seal the cuticles
  • Never use hot water or harsh shampoos
  • Allow hair to air dry or use low heat on dryer
Styling Tips
  • Preserve the natural wave with curl-defining creams
  • Use heat protectant spray before any heat styling
  • Straighten on low to medium heat if desired
  • Deep condition weekly for optimal moisture
  • Use a silk pillowcase to minimize friction
Storage & Maintenance
  • Store bundles on a hanger or in a silk bag
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and extreme heat
  • Apply leave-in conditioner before storage
  • Detangle gently from ends to roots with wide-tooth comb
  • Avoid excessive brushing to prevent shedding
Products to Use & Avoid
  • GOOD: Sulfate-free products, moisturizing shampoos, protein treatments
  • GOOD: Leave-in conditioners, hair oils, silk pillowcases
  • AVOID: Sulfate-based shampoos, chlorine water, bleaching
  • AVOID: Excessive heat styling, brushing wet hair, tight protective styles
