Our Wavy Curly Bundles feature 100% raw human hair with a beautiful blend of wavy and curly textures. Single donor, cuticle-aligned hair that maintains its natural wave and curl pattern. Perfect for creating voluminous, textured styles.

With proper care, these bundles can last 2-5+ years. The hair can be colored, permed, and styled to match your preferences. No synthetic fibers—just pure, premium quality.

Washing: Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Wash in lukewarm water, gently removing product from roots to ends. Avoid excessive rubbing.

Deep Conditioning: Apply deep conditioning treatment weekly to maintain softness and shine. Leave in for 15-20 minutes.

Styling: Air dry or use a diffuser on low heat. Avoid high heat as it can damage the curl pattern. Use curl-defining products to enhance wave and curl definition.

Shipping: All orders ship within 2-3 business days. Domestic orders arrive within 5-7 business days. International shipping available.

Returns: We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be unused and in original condition. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.
