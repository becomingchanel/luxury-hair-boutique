Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about our premium hair extensions, ordering, shipping, and care.

Ordering

How do I place an order?
Ordering is simple. Browse our collection, select your desired bundles, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately with your order details and tracking information once shipped.
Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?
Yes! We offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact our wholesale team at wholesale@luxuryhairboutique.com for details, minimum order requirements, and custom pricing. We work with stylists, salons, and resellers.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Can I place a custom order?
Absolutely! We offer custom orders for specific lengths, colors, textures, and quantities. Contact our customer service team and we'll work with you to create your perfect bundle.

Shipping

What is the processing time?
All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. This means your hair will be carefully packed and handed to our shipping carrier during this window. Processing times do not include weekends or holidays.
How long does standard shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days after processing. For faster delivery, we offer Express shipping which arrives in 1-2 business days. Shipping times are estimates and may vary based on your location.
What is Express shipping?
Express shipping guarantees delivery within 1-2 business days after processing. This option is perfect if you need your hair quickly. Express shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide! International orders take 7-14 business days depending on your location. Customs and import duties may apply and are the responsibility of the customer. You'll receive a tracking number to monitor your package.
How much does shipping cost?
Domestic shipping is FREE on all orders over $150. Orders under $150 are charged standard shipping rates starting at $10. International shipping costs vary by location. All shipping rates are calculated and displayed at checkout.
How do I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number and a direct link to monitor your package in real-time. You can also log into your account and check your order status at any time.

Returns

What is your return policy?
We offer a 14-day return window from the date of delivery. Items must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging with all tags attached. Once your return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
What are the conditions for returns?
Items must be unopened and in their original packaging with all tags intact. The hair should show no signs of use, styling, or damage. Bundles that appear used or damaged may not be eligible for return.
Can I exchange my hair?
Yes! If your hair doesn't meet your expectations or you'd like a different texture or length, we offer exchanges. Simply contact our customer service within 14 days of delivery with your request.
How long does a refund take?
Once we receive your return and verify the condition, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. Depending on your bank, the funds may take an additional 1-3 business days to appear in your account.
Are there any non-returnable items?
Final sale items, clearance bundles, and custom orders are typically non-returnable. These will be clearly marked on the product page at time of purchase.
How do I start a return?
Contact our customer service team at returns@luxuryhairboutique.com with your order number. We'll provide you with a prepaid return shipping label and return instructions. Once received, we'll inspect and process your refund.

Hair Care

How do I care for my extensions?
Use sulfate-free products, wash in cool water, apply deep conditioning treatments weekly, and always use a heat protectant before styling. Detangle gently with a wide-tooth comb, and avoid chlorine. We offer a complete hair care guide on our HAIR GUIDE page.
How long will my hair extensions last?
With proper care, our premium raw hair extensions can last 2 to 5+ years. The lifespan depends on styling frequency, heat usage, product quality, and maintenance. Regular deep conditioning and gentle handling extend longevity significantly.
Can I color or bleach my extensions?
Yes! Our 100% raw human hair can be colored, bleached, and styled like natural hair. However, we recommend consulting a professional colorist for best results. Coloring will require more frequent deep conditioning to maintain quality and shine.
Can I style my hair with heat?
Absolutely! Our hair can be blow-dried, flat-ironed, and curled. Always use a heat protectant spray before applying heat, and never apply extreme heat directly to the hair. Allow hair to cool completely before handling to prevent damage.
How do I prevent tangling?
Detangle gently before washing, store hair with bundles together, use conditioning treatments regularly, and sleep with hair braided or in a protective style. Avoid sleeping with wet hair and always use a wide-tooth comb rather than a brush.
What products should I use?
Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners designed for extensions or sensitive hair. Avoid products with silicones or harsh chemicals. Deep condition weekly, use leave-in conditioners, and apply hair oil to the ends. See our HAIR GUIDE for specific product recommendations.

Products

What hair types do you offer?
We offer a wide range of textures including Straight, Body Wave, Loose Wave, Deep Wave, Curly, and Coily. Each texture is available in multiple lengths and colors to match your needs and preferences.
What lengths are available?
Our bundles come in lengths from 10 inches to 32 inches. Longer lengths are available through custom orders. All lengths are measured from the root to the tip when hair is straightened.
How many colors do you have?
We offer over 20 natural color shades ranging from jet black to golden blonde. All colors are 100% human hair with realistic undertones. You can request color swatches before ordering to ensure a perfect match.
What's the difference between closures and frontals?
Closures are 4x4 or 5x5 inches and cover the crown area. Frontals are 13x4 inches and cover the entire hairline from ear to ear. Frontals provide more coverage and styling versatility for creating full wigs or install styles.
What are K-tips and how are they installed?
K-tips are pre-bonded hair extensions with a flat tip for fusion installation. They're applied individually using heat to bond the tip to your natural hair. K-tips last 2-3 months before requiring maintenance and can be reused multiple times.
How many bundles do I need?
The number of bundles needed depends on your natural hair density, desired fullness, and length. Typically, 2-3 bundles provide good volume, while 4-6 bundles create very full, thick hair. We recommend consulting with a stylist for personalized advice.
